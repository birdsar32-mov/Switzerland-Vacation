Experiences

Are you perhaps wondering what you may want to do while you are in Switzerland? Well, look no further! Below is a list of foods to try and places to see!

One thing that I, personally, would most love to do in Switzerland is take a scenic walk around the Lake Oeschinen Loop

Foods to Try

  • Cheese Fondue
  • Raclette
  • Rösti (a crispy potato dish)
  • Zürcher Geschnetzeltes (veal in cream sauce)
  • Älplermagronen (alpine macaroni and cheese)
  • Basler Läckerli, Bündner Nusstorte (nut cake)

Places to Visit

Historical Center

Alstadt, Zurich's historical center is a beautiful mix of old and new. It's home to iconic churches like the twin towers of Grossmunster as well as Fraumunster, which is famous for its stained glass windows. You can also climb up Lindenhof Hill for a bird's eye view of the town and walk the pedestrianized streets of Niederdorf and Limmatquai—they are lively with shops by day and packed with nightlife as the sun goes down.

Chapel Bridge

The Chapel Bridge is one of the oldest and longest covered wooden bridges in Europe, as well as its rich history and unique paintings depicting scenes from Swiss history. It is an iconic symbol of Lucerne.

Lake Oeschinen Loop

This circular route to this breathtakingly beautiful mountain lake begins at the mountain station of the Oeschinensee gondola lift, which begins in Kandersteg. It is fed by the glacial streams of the Blüemlisalphorn, Oeschinenhorn, Fründenhorn and Doldenhorn.There are also a few mountain huts along the route, where hikers can recharge their batteries and really take in the Oeschinen area.
