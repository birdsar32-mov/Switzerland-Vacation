Trip Preparation

To prepare for a trip to Switzerland, there are many things to consider. Below is a list of steps one might consider taking before taking an international trip.

  1. Determine your budget and interests
  2. Book flights, accomodations, and any tours or excursions well in advance
  3. Apply for a travel visa if necessary
  4. Purchase travel insurance and aquire local currency (Swiss Francs)
  5. Pack your bags
    Bathroom necessities
    Toothbrush, toothpaste, hairbrush, hair dryer, makeup, skincare, etc.
    Clothing suitable for the weather you plan to travel in
    • Summer - Tank-tops, t-shirts, shorts, skirts, tennishoes, etc.
    • Fall - Cardigans, jackets, jeans, tennishoes/boots, etc.
    • Winter - Coat, thermal underwear, snow boots, hat, gloves, etc.
    • Spring - Cardigans, light jackets, jeans, leggings, tennishoes/boots, etc.
    Electronic Devices
    Phone, charger, earbuds/headphones, charger, Laptop, charger, Camera, charger
    Optional
    Travel journal/scrapbook
  6. download relevant travel apps for public transport, and familiarize yourself with Swiss cultural norms
